Caption

Scanning electron micrograph (SEM) of Shigella dysenteriae, Gram-negative, enteric, facultatively anaerobic, rod prokaryote; causes bacterial dysentery. This species is most often found in water contaminated with human faeces. Magnification: x2,200 when shortest axis printed at 25 millimetres.
